Company Introduction

Licensed Certification Authority

Established in 1999, MSC Trustgate.com Sdn Bhd is licensed by the Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC) under the Digital Signatures Act 1997 to issue and manage digital certificates and digital identities based on Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) for the Malaysian market.

Our core business involves issuing digital certificates and providing digital signatures that secure e-government systems, public services, and the nation’s critical infrastructure.

Our Solutions

  • Web Authentication (TLS/SSL)
  • Digital Identity / Digital Certificate
  • Digital Date & Timestamping
  • Digital Seal
  • Digital Signature
